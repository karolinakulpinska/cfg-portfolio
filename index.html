Art Projects

Picture of Evil - illustration

70x50cm, acrylics, ballpen, colored pencils on cardboard. A part of my BA diploma project, Academy of Fine Arts in Łódź, Poland. The first in series of 4 cover illustrations for Graham Masterton books.

Spirit Jump - illustration

70x50cm, acrylics, ballpen, colored pencils on cardboard. The third one in the series of 4 cover illustrations for Graham Masterton books. It's an illustration for a short story collection titled Faces of fear, inspired by the particular one - Spirit Jump. This is my interpretation of an abandoned subway tunnel in which the story's twist ending takes it's place.

The house that Jack built - illustration

70x50cm, acrylics, ballpen, colored pencils on cardboard. The second one in the series of 4 cover illustrations for Graham Masterton books. It's my take on Walhalla - old, majestic and mysterious residency - the main cause of all Bellmans' problems. My interpretation is highly inspired by the 19th-century neo-Gothic architecture, as described in the book.

Bridal Suite - illustration

70x50cm, acrylics, ballpen, colored pencils on cardboard. The last one in the series of 4 cover illustrations for Graham Masterton books. This one's for another short story titled Bridal Suite that is a part of a bigger collection - Flights of fear.
